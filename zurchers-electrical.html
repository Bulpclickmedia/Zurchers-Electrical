Who We Are

TRUSTED SINCE
1992

Zurcher's Electrical & Electronics has been delivering quality electrical services to Port Elizabeth and the greater Gqeberha region for over three decades. What started as a small local electrician has grown into one of the most trusted names in the Eastern Cape — backed by nearly 5,000 five-star Google reviews.

We handle everything from a tripped circuit breaker to a full industrial installation, and our solar & backup power division is among the most experienced in the region.
